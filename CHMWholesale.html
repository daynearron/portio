Wholesale lending made simple, personal, and quick.

Overview

Commerce Home Mortgage Wholesale values new perspectives and creative ideas

Commerce Home Mortgage Wholesale aspires to treat wholesale lending with a start-up mentality, by constantly re-evaluating antiquated processes full of red tape and rigid structure that slows down big banks, bringing fresh new ideas and experiences to their clients.

The Challenge

Commerce Home Mortgage Wholesale knew what they wanted, but how:

  • Refresh current branding.

  • Optimize day-to-day workflow of key employees.

  • Strategize ways to increase lead acquisition.

Roles

The Process

Divide and Conquer

We got started by assigning tasks based on individual strengths. I began with interviewing and shadowing primary stakeholders seeking qualitative data on the current procedures to identify pain points and exploring possible solutions.

Field research

What I learned

  • Broker Forms:

    The lengthiest part of a brokers day-to-day is completing a Broker Agreement Package, a document containing all the necessary forms needed to move forward on submitting a loan. This could take 35 minutes to an hour to complete.

  • Turn times:

    Normally a broker would need to reach out to several different sources to get the turn-around times for specific functions.

  • Rate-Sheets:

    Brokers rely on excel documents to find the most suitable Loan Program for their client, because these are excel documents that need to be updated daily it became a nightmare for version control plus they can be quite lengthy.

  • Marketing Materials:

    Per information obtained through the marketing VP, we found email and print campaigns have helped generate millions in originated loans but the process of creating customized collateral was inconvenient.
