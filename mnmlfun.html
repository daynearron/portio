MNML FUN

Branding and collateral for local techno party.

Overview

Monthly Techno Party

MNMLFUN is a local Techno Party that occurs once a month at San Franciscos Underground SF. I teamed up with the resident DJs to create a branding system and monthly collateral to entice local residents as well as visitors to come to their show.
